This is an energy-rich take-away breakfast for those mornings when things are a little less relaxed. The bars are also delicious for the lunch box or as a snack. We sometimes exchange the dried cranberries and raisains for dates or another 
dried fruit or add some almonds and pumpkin seeds. So don't be afraid to add some of your favourite nuts and seeds as well! 

<ResponsiveGrid>
  <Paragraph title={"INGREDIENTS"}>
    - 75 g rolled oats
    - 125 g wholewheat flour
    - 125 g sugar
    - 1/4 tsp baking powder
    - 1/4 tsp salt
    - 1 tsp ground cinnamon
    - pinch of ground nutmeg/cardamom
    - 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
    - 2 tbsp dried cranberries/raisins
    - 2 tbsp sunflower seeds
    - 125 ml coconut oil
  </Paragraph>

  <Paragraph title={"PREPARATION"} >
    1. Preheat the oven to 170°C and grease a 20 x 20 cm baking tin.
    2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl. Add the coconut oil and mix until the mixture is crumbly.
    3. Press the mixture into the baking tin and bake for 30-35 minutes.
    4. Let the bars cool down and cut into 12 bars.
    5. Enjoy!
  </Paragraph>
</ResponsiveGrid>

The bars can be stored in an airtight container for up to 7 days.
